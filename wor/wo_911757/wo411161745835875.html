1.1.2.1 Functional Architecture

  1. WCS/TMA subsystem is in charge of the:
    1. Elaboration of the target motion analysis on sensors/system tracks
    2. Management and launching of the tactical weapons.
  2. To help the various operators in the fulfilment of their mission, it permits also the following functions (pure tactical domain or transverse tasks):
    1. On board training
    2. Recording and Replay of launching phases
    3. Supervision of the
      WCSWeapon Control Subsystem
      equipments and torpedoes.
